Razer Deathadder 2013

This mouse has a good egronomic feel and is used by profesional gamers all over the globe. It has well designed mouse skates that are durable and smooth and has two programable buttons to suite your gaming needs.

This mouse has 6 programable buttons which can be very useful in specific games or causual web brousing. Though it may be heavy and look bulky, it still has a good feel but the mouse skates should be replaced as they tear easily.
